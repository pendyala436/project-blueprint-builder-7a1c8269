================================================================================
                        APP INSTALLATION GUIDE
                       Meow Meow - Friend & Chat
================================================================================

This guide explains how to install the Meow Meow app on all devices and 
operating systems. The app is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that can be 
installed directly from your browser.


================================================================================
                              iOS (iPhone & iPad)
================================================================================

Requirements: Safari browser (required - other browsers don't support this)

Steps:
------
1. Open Safari browser on your iPhone or iPad

2. Navigate to the Meow Meow app website

3. Tap the SHARE button at the bottom of the screen
   • It looks like a square with an arrow pointing up (⬆️)

4. Scroll down in the share menu

5. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
   • It has a plus (+) icon next to it

6. Enter a name for the app (or keep the default)

7. Tap "Add" in the top right corner

8. Done! The app icon will appear on your home screen

Note: Chrome, Firefox, and other browsers on iOS do NOT support PWA 
installation. You MUST use Safari.


================================================================================
                              Android
================================================================================

Requirements: Chrome, Samsung Internet, Edge, or Opera browser

Option A - Automatic Prompt:
----------------------------
1. Open Chrome (or compatible browser) on your Android device

2. Navigate to the Meow Meow app website

3. You should see an "Install" or "Add to Home Screen" popup automatically

4. Tap "Install"

5. Done! The app icon will appear on your home screen


Option B - Manual Installation (if no popup appears):
----------------------------------------------------
1. Open Chrome on your Android device

2. Navigate to the Meow Meow app website

3. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top right corner

4. Tap "Install App" or "Add to Home Screen"

5. Tap "Install" to confirm

6. Done! The app icon will appear on your home screen


================================================================================
                              Windows
================================================================================

Requirements: Chrome, Edge, or Opera browser

Option A - Address Bar Install:
-------------------------------
1. Open Chrome or Edge browser

2. Navigate to the Meow Meow app website

3. Look for the install icon (⊕) in the address bar (right side)

4. Click the install icon

5. Click "Install" in the popup dialog

6. Done! The app will open in its own window and be added to Start Menu


Option B - Browser Menu:
------------------------
1. Open Chrome or Edge browser

2. Navigate to the Meow Meow app website

3. Click the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top right corner

4. Click "Install Meow Meow" or "Apps > Install this site as an app"

5. Click "Install" to confirm

6. Done! The app is now installed


================================================================================
                              macOS (Mac)
================================================================================

Requirements: Chrome, Edge, or Safari browser

Using Chrome or Edge:
--------------------
1. Open Chrome or Edge browser

2. Navigate to the Meow Meow app website

3. Look for the install icon (⊕) in the address bar

4. Click the install icon

5. Click "Install" in the popup dialog

6. Done! The app will be added to your Applications folder


Using Safari (macOS Sonoma 14+):
-------------------------------
1. Open Safari browser

2. Navigate to the Meow Meow app website

3. Click "File" in the menu bar

4. Click "Add to Dock"

5. Done! The app will be added to your Dock


================================================================================
                              Linux
================================================================================

Requirements: Chrome, Edge, or Chromium browser

Steps:
------
1. Open Chrome, Edge, or Chromium browser

2. Navigate to the Meow Meow app website

3. Look for the install icon (⊕) in the address bar (right side)
   OR click the three-dot menu (⋮)

4. Click "Install Meow Meow" or "Install app"

5. Click "Install" to confirm

6. Done! The app will be available in your applications menu


================================================================================
                              Chrome OS (Chromebook)
================================================================================

Requirements: Chrome browser (built-in)

Steps:
------
1. Open Chrome browser

2. Navigate to the Meow Meow app website

3. Click the install icon (⊕) in the address bar

4. Click "Install"

5. Done! The app will appear in your app launcher

================================================================================
                           TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Problem: Install option not appearing
Solution: 
• Make sure you're using a supported browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari)
• Refresh the page and wait a few seconds
• Clear browser cache and try again

Problem: "Add to Home Screen" not available on iOS
Solution:
• You MUST use Safari - other browsers don't support this on iOS
• Make sure iOS is updated to the latest version

Problem: App not updating
Solution:
• Close the app completely
• Reopen the app - it will check for updates
• Or clear the app cache in your device settings

Problem: Push notifications not working
Solution:
• Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings
• Grant notification permission when prompted in the app


================================================================================
                              UNINSTALLING
================================================================================

iOS:
----
Long-press the app icon > Tap "Remove App" > Tap "Delete App"

Android:
--------
Long-press the app icon > Drag to "Uninstall" or tap "Uninstall"

Windows:
--------
Settings > Apps > Find "Meow Meow" > Click "Uninstall"

macOS:
------
Open Finder > Applications > Drag app to Trash

Linux:
------
Use your distribution's app manager or remove from applications menu
